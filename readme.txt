== Frequently Asked Questions ==
 
= How much is it to access the analytics dashboard =
 
The price is $10 per month.
 
= Why should I use this instead of other tools? =
 
Our dashboard is very simple, no need for complex instructions. 

= Can you customize my dashboard to include specific items I would like to have? =
 
Yes, our product is always evolving, we encourage active involvement with your ideas.
